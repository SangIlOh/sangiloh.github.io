About me

I am working for Selvas AI located in Seoul, Korea (I am replacing my military service duty here). At the company, I am developing methods for detecting and classifying lesions from biomedical images (e.g., X-ray, ultrasonic, and so on) based on machine learning and computer vision algorithms from Oct/2017. I graduated my B.S.(Feb/2015) and M.S.(Feb/2017) in Media Engineering at Catholic University of Korea, under the supervision of Hang-Bong Kang.

Major Research

Object Detection and Classification: Object Detection in Autonomous Driving Systems; Object Detection in Robotics; Detect lesions from X-ray and ultrasonic images;

Object Tracking: Occupancy Grid Filtering in the Driving Situation; Multi-object Tracking for Driving Systems; 3D Object Tracking;

Semantic Segmentation: Segment lesions from X-ray and ultrasonic images;
